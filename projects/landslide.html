Workflow structure

The workflow below highlights the main technical stages instead of retelling the tutorial line by line. That makes the analysis logic easier to review quickly.

Stage 01

Building extraction from imagery

Aerial imagery was processed in ArcGIS Online using a pretrained deep learning model to generate building footprints for the study area.

Stage 02

Susceptibility model setup

Environmental raster inputs were prepared as the analytical basis for the susceptibility surface, including elevation, soil types, land use, and distance to rivers.

Stage 03

Susceptibility surface creation

Raster analysis was used to classify the landscape into landslide susceptibility classes and establish the broader spatial risk context across the island.

Stage 04

Exposure overlay and delivery

The building layer was compared with the susceptibility surface to identify exposed structures, and the final outputs were delivered through a web map and Instant App.

This project is strongest when read as an integration workflow: imagery-based extraction, raster modeling, and final infrastructure exposure interpretation in one online analysis pipeline.
